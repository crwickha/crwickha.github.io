# About

Hi, I'm Craig.

I'm a Technical Solutions Architect with over 16 years of experience working in the ever-changing IT space. My background spans networking, automation, cloud, and security, with a particular focus on fabric technologies such as BGP EVPN and software-defined access. Over the years I've become especially passionate about automation and the growing role of AI in simplifying complex systems.

This blog is where I document and share solutions, ideas, and experiments I find interesting in the world of networking and infrastructure. Much of the content focuses on network automation, APIs, DevOps-style tooling, and platforms such as Cisco and Meraki. Some posts will be hands-on tutorials, while others will explore practical insights from real-world deployments and projects.

The goal is simple: share useful things I've learned along the way. If you're a network engineer, developer, or someone exploring the transition from traditional networking into automation and programmable infrastructure, you'll likely find something here.
